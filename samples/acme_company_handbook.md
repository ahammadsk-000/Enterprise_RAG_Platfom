# Acme Corp — Company Handbook

## About Acme
Acme Corp was founded in 2015 by Jane Doe and Raj Patel in Boston, Massachusetts.
Acme builds cloud security software for mid-market and enterprise customers. The
company moved its headquarters from Boston to Austin, Texas in 2023 to be closer to
its engineering talent pool. As of 2024 Acme employs roughly 480 people across three
offices: Austin, Boston, and a remote-first engineering hub in Lisbon.

The current CEO is Jane Doe. Raj Patel serves as Chief Technology Officer. Maria Lopez
joined as Chief Financial Officer in 2022, having previously worked at Northwind
Analytics.

## Products
Acme sells two flagship products:

- **SentinelGuard** — a real-time network intrusion detection platform that uses
  behavioral analytics to flag anomalous traffic. SentinelGuard is sold per-seat with
  annual contracts and is the company's largest revenue line.
- **DataShield Cloud** — an encryption product that protects data at rest and in
  transit. DataShield Cloud came to Acme through the 2021 acquisition of DataShield,
  a Seattle startup founded by Tom Becker.

A third product, **PolicyForge**, is in private beta and lets compliance teams author
and enforce access policies as code.

## Company history
- 2015 — Acme founded in Boston by Jane Doe and Raj Patel.
- 2017 — SentinelGuard 1.0 released; first enterprise customer signed.
- 2019 — Series B funding round led by Harbor Ventures.
- 2021 — Acquired DataShield (Seattle) to expand the data-protection portfolio.
- 2023 — Headquarters relocated to Austin.
- 2024 — PolicyForge entered private beta; headcount reached ~480.

## Working at Acme
Acme is remote-first. Employees may work from any of the three offices or from home.
Core collaboration hours are 10:00–15:00 in the employee's local time zone. New hires
complete a two-week onboarding program covering security training, the codebase, and a
shadowing rotation with the Customer Success team.
